WHO WE ARE

We are an open community of web developers, who have come together under the same banner.

Our values are all aligned along moral and professional lines. Anyone can become a member of Code Pod.

Our community emerged from a desire to support each other and share resources and knowledge. Central to our foundations is a commitment to lifelong learning and self development.

We code together regularly.

Every Code Pod member is encouraged to activitly take part in the community; running meetups, creating resources for use, and bringing their projects into the group for advice or to find people to share the work with.

We also have a pool of varied, ongoing projects for developers at different levels; the projects are based around topics individuals want to learn more about.

Our Goals

To continue to grow as a community and expand our activities

To promote and facilitate the sharing of projects and knowledge

To facilitate varied community driven activities such as meetups, hackathons and remote hangouts on specialist topics.
